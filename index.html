Top three activities to do at Queretaro

Roam Around the Monuments of Queretaro’s Historic Center

The Historic Monuments Zone of Queretaro is what garnered the city its Unesco-listed status.
Ambling around the historic centro is a “must” for things to do in Queretaro.

Tour the Queretaro Wine Region

There are actually some good wineries surrounding the city of Queretaro.
This state boasts Mexico's second biggest wine region (after Baja California).

Hike Up One of the World’s Largest Monoliths: Peña de Bernal

Venturing out to Bernal is one the best things to do in Queretaro for a day trip.
It only takes about an hour to reach the small colonial town of Bernal, where a massive rock seemingly sprouts right up from the ground.
